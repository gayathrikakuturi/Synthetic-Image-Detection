Under the Hood

How the System Works

The system uses MobileNetV2 to detect synthetic imagery by analyzing low-level and high-level visual features.

The model evaluates image patterns, textures, edge behavior, and lighting inconsistencies common in AI-generated content.

GradCAM highlights the specific regions responsible for the prediction, making results interpretable and trustworthy.
